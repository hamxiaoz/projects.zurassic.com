How It Works

  • Online meetings — Just click Start. Your mic captures primarily your voice. The app transcribes speech in real time and calculates words per minute over a rolling window.
  • In-person meetings — Click "Enroll My Voice" first. The app learns your pitch profile and only counts words when your voice is detected, ignoring other speakers.
  • Alerts — A visual banner and/or a soft chime plays when you exceed the threshold. The alert resets after 10 seconds of normal-paced speech.
  • Browser support — Requires Chrome or Edge for real-time speech recognition. Safari has limited support.
