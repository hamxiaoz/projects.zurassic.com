How it works
  1. Sit or stand at your desk and click Start Monitoring — the camera detects your presence and starts the timer
  2. Step away from the camera and the timer pauses automatically
  3. When you've been at your desk too long, the alarm fires — screen flashes and an alert sound plays
  4. To dismiss: hold an open palm toward the camera for 3 seconds, click the Dismiss button, or simply step out of frame
