Flow Break uses computer vision to detect when you're at your desk and reminds you to step away — before hours slip by unnoticed.

⏱
Sit Timer & Alarm
Computer vision watches your desk. Set a sitting limit and Flow Break sounds an alarm — screen flash, custom alarm tone — when time's up.
✋
Gesture Dismiss
Hold an open palm toward the camera for 3 seconds. Anime-style effects charge up and burst as the timer counts down. No keyboard needed.
🎬
Movie-Inspired FX
13 real-time video filters — Terminator HUD, Predator thermal, Night Vision, Amber CRT, Apple II and more. Pick your vibe.
📷
Requires Camera
Flow Break uses your webcam to detect presence and read hand gestures. No account or setup needed.
🔒
100% Private
Everything runs locally in your browser. No video is recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.
How it works
  1. Sit at your desk and click Start Monitoring — the camera detects your presence and starts the sit timer
  2. Step away from the camera and the timer pauses automatically
  3. When you've been sitting too long, the alarm fires — screen flashes and an alert sound plays
  4. To dismiss: hold an open palm toward the camera for 3 seconds, or simply step out of frame
